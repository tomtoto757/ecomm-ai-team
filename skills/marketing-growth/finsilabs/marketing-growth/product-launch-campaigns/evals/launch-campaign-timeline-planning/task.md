# Go-to-Market Campaign Plan for Wireless Headphones Launch

## Problem/Feature Description

Apex Audio is a consumer electronics brand preparing to launch its first premium wireless headphones — the Apex Arc — in six weeks. The founder has bootstrapped the company and has a modest but engaged email list of 3,000 subscribers and relationships with five audio-focused content creators on YouTube and TikTok. The previous product release was a quiet affair: they simply listed the product on their site and sent one announcement email. It sold slowly for weeks before gaining traction.

This time, the marketing lead wants a structured, multi-channel campaign plan that maximizes the pre-launch buildup, makes their loyalty customers feel special, and keeps momentum going for at least a month after the product goes on sale. The plan needs to cover email, social, influencer, and paid advertising channels — and it needs to be specific enough that the marketing team can start executing immediately.

## Output Specification

Produce a campaign plan document named `launch-plan.md` that includes:

- A detailed timeline with specific dates or T-minus notation for each channel action
- For each phase, list all planned touchpoints with channel, action, and audience
- Guidance on influencer brief requirements and scheduling
- Waitlist goals and advice on when to start building
- Paid social campaign sequencing across all three phases
- Post-launch content and engagement activities through day 30
- A note on inventory planning considerations

The plan should be concrete and actionable — the marketing team should be able to hand this to any contractor and have them execute it.
